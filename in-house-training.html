In-House iOS Training.

Shiny Development can tailor an iPhone and iPad development training course to your organisation’s specific needs.

Our bespoke training courses provide a cost effective way to bring your whole team up to speed, allowing them all to contribute to your development project from day one.

Example Course Overview

  • Day 1: Introduction to iPhone Development and the Objective-C Language.
  • Day 2: Building a basic iPhone application, Interface Builder, Views and View Controllers & Table Views.
  • Day 3: Client Specific topics (e.g. video streaming, animation, client / server issues), Deployment and Provisioning and The App Store.
  • Day 4: Further client specific topics (or, build a complete iPhone application, Tweet Trends from start to finish in a day.

About the Trainer

Our in-house training courses are designed and delivered by Dave Verwer, owner of Shiny Development and developer of Balloons! and several other successful iPhone applications. As Dave is primarily an iPhone and iPad developer, rather than a full-time trainer and brings direct experience in designing, developing, marketing and shipping successful apps.

Prerequisites

To get the most out of this course, all trainees should be proficient developers with a good knowledge of a modern, object oriented language such as Java, C#, Python, Ruby or C++. However, no knowledge of Objective-C or the iPhone SDK is required.
