Preparing a Translucent Screenshot with Status Magic

As of version 1.7, Status Magic now includes support for translucent status bars in app screenshots. However, screenshots taken from apps which make use of translucent status bars must be captured in a slightly different way before they can be processed.

Screenshots captured from an app which uses a transparent status bar must have the status bar excluded from the capture area when the screenshot is taken. This is not possible by capturing screenshots on the device or simulator and the screenshot must be taken by calling code internally from within your app.
