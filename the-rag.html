The RAG

The RAG is shake-me-up box of delights for the iPhone that generates thousands of fresh learning challenges. Give the RAG (Random Activity Generator) a shake, the cards spin and you are presented with a random activity to do.

Primed with over 50,000 possible combinations the RAG contains a wonderful storehouse of delightful calls to action – it will take you to the tipping point between view and do.

About The RAG

The aim is to provoke thought, activity and a deeper understanding all in the innocent guise of having some learning fun, facing a challenge and making something.

“A fruit machine for learning.”

Merlin John, UK technology guru and writer

Ideal for:

  • People of all ages wanting to set themselves a quick bit of learning
  • Teachers looking for a few thousand ideas
  • Parents wanting to make learning more fun
  • Home educators who need a bit of inspiration

Watch the demo to get an idea of how it works or visit http://theragis.us to see the work produced by others when they undertook as RAG Challenge. Send us ideas for future RAGs or contact us if you want to discuss your own custom RAG.
