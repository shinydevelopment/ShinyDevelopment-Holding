Explore Flickr

Want to see the most interesting, vibrant and beautiful images on flickr.com right on your iPad or iPhone?

Explore Flickr will show you stunning photography from around the world in perfect high definition.

Buy Explore Flickr Now!

Standard Features

Every day, millions of new photographs are uploaded to flickr and the absolute best of them are chosen to be on the “Explore” section of the site. Explore Flickr for iPad and iPhone allows you to browse those images in perfect high definition.

  • Browse hundreds of the top daily images from flickr.com.
  • Find beautiful wallpaper and lock screen images and download them direct to your photo library.
  • Instantly see photo title and tag details, or browse all details and photo comments right on flickr.com from within the app.
  • An entirely new set of photographs refreshed daily for a constant stream of fresh photography!

Premium Features

But why limit yourself to just the photography that flickr picks every day? Upgrade to premium from within the app and search more than 5 billion photos unlocking all of the content that flickr has to offer.

  • Text Search - Search for any keyword on flickr.
  • Explore by Tag - Click on any tag to explore further.
  • Search History - Keep track of your favourite searches.
  • Slideshow - View a rolling slideshow of all displayed images on .

Requirements

Explore Flickr works on any iOS device. The only restriction is that you are running iOS 4.0 on an iPhone or iPod touch or iOS 3.2 on the iPad.
