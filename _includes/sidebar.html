About Dave Verwer

Dave is an iPhone developer and trainer and is the creator of Balloons! for iPhone which has been ranked #1 in the iTunes "What's Hot" featured application around the world.

Dave has been developing with the iPhone SDK since it was first released, has developed several successful applications and trained over a hundred other people to do the same!
